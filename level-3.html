句式结构 (jùshì jiégòu) Sentence structures

你家有几个人？
Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ gè rén?
How many people are there in your family?

我家有三口人，爸爸，妈妈和我。
Wǒ jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén, bàba, māma hé wǒ.
There are three people in my family, dad, mum and me.

我爱我的爸爸和妈妈。
Wǒ ài wǒ de bàba hé māma.
I love my dad and mum.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

你家有宠物吗？
Nǐ jiā yǒu chǒngwù ma?
Are there pets in your home?

是的。我家有宠物。
Shì de. Wǒ jiā yǒu chǒngwù.
Yes. There are pets in my home.

你有宠物吗？
Nǐ yǒu chǒngwù ma?
Do you have pets?

是的，我有宠物。
Shì de, wǒ yǒu chǒngwù.
Yes, I have pets.

你有什么宠物？
Nǐ yǒu shénme chǒngwù?
What pets do you have?

我有一只狗。
Wǒ yǒu yīzhī gǒu.
I have a dog.

你家有几个人？
Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ gè rén?
How many people are there in your family?

我家有五口人，外公，外婆，爸爸，妈妈和我。
Wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén, wàigōng, wàipó, bàba, māma hé wǒ.
There are five people in my family, grandfather, grandmother, dad, mum and me.

我爱我的家人。
Wǒ ài wǒ de jiārén.
I love my family.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

你有什么爱好？
Nǐ yǒu shénme àihào?
What hobbies do you have?

（我喜欢）看书，游永和上网。
(Wǒ xǐhuān) kànshū, yóuyǒng hé shàngwǎng.
(I like) reading books, swimming and surfing the Internet.

你的（兴趣）爱好是什么？
Nǐ de (xìngqù) àihào shì shénme?
What are your hobbies?

（我的兴趣爱好是）看书，游永和上网。
(Wǒ de xìngqù àihào shì) kànshū, yóuyǒng hé shàngwǎng.
(My hobbies) are reading books, swimming and surfing the Internet.

(I like) playing football and pingpong and jogging.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

你这个星期六有空吗？我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。
Nǐ zhège xīngqī liù yǒu kòng ma? \
Wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì.
Will you be free this Saturday? \
I want to invite you to attend my birthday party.

（是的，）我有空。谢谢你的生日聚会邀请。我（很）高兴接受你的邀请。
(Shì de,) wǒ yǒu kòng. Xièxie nǐ de shēngrì jùhuì yāoqǐng. \
Wǒ (hěn) gāoxìng jiēshòu nǐ de yāoqǐng.
Yes, I will be free. \
Thanks for the invitation to your birthday party. \
I am (very) happy to accept your invitation.

你这个周六有空吗？我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。
Nǐ zhège zhōu liù yǒu kòng ma? \
Wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì.
Will you be free this Saturday? \
I want to invite you to attend my birthday party.

对不起，我没（有）空，我有安排。谢谢你的生日聚会邀请。
Duìbùqī, wǒ méi (yǒu) kòng, wǒ yǒu ānpái. \
Xièxie nǐ de shēngrì jùhuì yāoqǐng.
No, I am sorry that I will not be free and I have an arrangement. \
Thanks for the invitation to your birthday party.
