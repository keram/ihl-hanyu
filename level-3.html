Lesson 1

主题 | zhǔtí | subject

家庭族员和宠物（一） Jiātíng chéngyuán hé chǒngwù (yī) Family members and pets (1)

词语 | cíyǔ | words

Table 1: 家庭成员 | jiātíng chéngyuán | family members
chinese pinyin english
爸爸 bàba father
妈妈 māma mother
哥哥 gēge elder brother
姐姐 jiějie elder sister
女儿 nǚ'ér daughter
儿子 érzi son
丈夫 zhàngfū husband
妻子 qīzi wife
爱人 àirén spouse
男人 nánrén man
女人 nǚrén woman
口 kǒu a measure word for family members

句式结构 | jù shì jiégòu | sentence structures

你家有几个人？ Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ gè rén? How many people are there in your family?

我家有三口人，爸爸，妈妈和我。 Wǒ jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén, bàba, māma hé wǒ. There are three people in my family, dad, mum and me.

我爱我的爸爸和妈妈。 Wǒ ài wǒ de bàba hé māma. I love my dad and mum.

Lesson 2

主题 | zhǔtí | subject

家庭族员和宠物（二） Jiātíng chéngyuán hé chǒngwù (èr) Family members and pets (2)

词语 | cíyǔ | words

Table 2: 家庭成员 | jiātíng chéngyuán | family members
chinese pinyin english
弟弟 dìdi younger brother
妹妹 mèimei younger sister
外公 wàigōng grandpa (mother's side)
外婆 wàipó grandma (mother's side)
爷爷 yéye father's father
奶奶 nǎinai father's mother
Table 3: 宠物 | chǒngwù | pets
chinese pinyin english
宠物 chǒngwù pet
猫 māo cat
狗 gǒu dog
蛇 shé snake
鸟 niǎo bird
兔子 tùzi rabbit
只 zhī a measure word for most animals
条 tiáo a measure word for long things
有 yǒu there be/to have
不用谢 bùyòng xiè you are welcome

句子 - 职业 | jùzi - zhíyè | sentences

你家有宠物吗？ Nǐ jiā yǒu chǒngwù ma? Are there pets in your home?

是的。我家有宠物。 Shì de. Wǒ jiā yǒu chǒngwù. Yes. There are pets in my home.

你有宠物吗？ Nǐ yǒu chǒngwù ma? Do you have pets?

是的，我有宠物。 Shì de, wǒ yǒu chǒngwù. Yes, I have pets.

你有什么宠物？ Nǐ yǒu shénme chǒngwù? What pets do you have?

我有一只狗。 Wǒ yǒu yīzhī gǒu. I have a dog.

你家有几个人？ Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ gè rén? How many people are there in your family?

我家有五口人，外公，外婆，爸爸，妈妈和我。 Wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén, wàigōng, wàipó, bàba, māma hé wǒ. There are five people in my family, grandfather, grandmother, dad, mum and me.

我爱我的家人。 Wǒ ài wǒ de jiārén. I love my family.

Lesson 3

主题 | zhǔtí | subject

爱好（一） Àihào (yī) Hobbies (1)

词语 | cíyǔ | words

chinese pinyin english
爱好 àihào hobby
看书 kànshū to read book
足球 zúqiú football
比赛 bǐsài match/game/competition
电影 diànyǐng movie/film
博物馆 bówùguǎn museum
游永 yóuyǒng swim/swimming
网球 wǎngqiú tennis
音乐 yīnyuè music
上网 shàngwǎng surf the internet
滑板 huábǎn skateboard
兴趣 xìngqù interest

句子 | jùzi | sentences

你有什么爱好？ Nǐ yǒu shénme àihào? What hobbies do you have?

（我喜欢）看书，游永和上网。 (Wǒ xǐhuān) kànshū, yóuyǒng hé shàngwǎng. (I like) reading books, swimming and surfing the Internet.

你的（兴趣）爱好是什么？ Nǐ de (xìngqù) àihào shì shénme? What are your hobbies?

（我的兴趣爱好是）看书，游永和上网。 (Wǒ de xìngqù àihào shì) kànshū, yóuyǒng hé shàngwǎng. (My hobbies) are reading books, swimming and surfing the Internet.

句子 | jùzi | sentences

你有那些（业余）爱好？ Nǐ yǒu nǎxiē (yèyú) àihào? What hobbies do you have?

看书，听音乐和打乒乓球。 Kànshū, tīng yīnyuè hé dǎ pīngpāngqiú. Reading books, listening to music and playing pingpong.

你喜欢什么运动？ Nǐ xǐhuān shénme yùndòng? What (kind of) sports do you like?

（我喜欢）踢足球，打乒乓球和慢跑。 (Wǒ xǐhuān) tī zúqiú, dǎ pīngpāng qiú hé mànpǎo. (I like) playing football and pingpong and jogging.

Lesson 6

主题 | zhǔtí | subject

谈亲访友 Tàngqīn fǎng yǒu Visiting home

词语 | cíyǔ | words

chinese pinyin english
有空 yǒu kòng have time
没空 méikòng not free/have no time
参加 cānjiā take part in/attend
安排 ān pái arrange/arrangement
对不起 duìbùqǐ sorry
邀请 yāoqǐng to invite
来 lái to come
去 qù to go
没关系 méiguānxì no problem/no worries
接（某人） jiē (mǒurén) to pick (someone) up
生日聚会 shēngrì jù huì birthday party
情柬 qǐngjiǎn invitation
站 zhàn stop/station
周 zhōu week
周六 zhōuliù Saturday

句子 | jùzi | sentences

你这个星期六有空吗？我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。 Nǐ zhège xīngqī liù yǒu kòng ma? Wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì. Will you be free this Saturday? I want to invite you to attend my birthday party.

（是的，）我有空。谢谢你的生日聚会邀请。我（很）高兴接受你的邀请。 (Shì de,) wǒ yǒu kòng. Xièxie nǐ de shēngrì jùhuì yāoqǐng. Wǒ (hěn) gāoxìng jiēshòu nǐ de yāoqǐng. Yes, I will be free. Thanks for the invitation to your birthday party. I am (very) happy to accept your invitation.

你这个周六有空吗？我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。 Nǐ zhège zhōu liù yǒu kòng ma? Wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì. Will you be free this Saturday? I want to invite you to attend my birthday party.

对不起，我没（有）空，我有安排。谢谢你的生日聚会邀请。 Duìbùqī, wǒ méi (yǒu) kòng, wǒ yǒu ānpái. Xièxie nǐ de shēngrì jùhuì yāoqǐng. No, I am sorry that I will not be free and I have an arrangement. Thanks for the invitation to your birthday party.
