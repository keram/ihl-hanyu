句式结构 (jù shì jiégòu) Sentence structures

请问我能坐这个椅子吗？ Qǐngwèn wǒ néng zuò zhège yǐzi ma? Can/Could I sit on this chair, please?

能，请坐。 Néng, qǐng zuò. Yes, sit down, please.

小狗去哪里了？/ 小狗在哪里？ Xiǎo gǒu qù nǎlǐle?/ Xiǎo gǒu zài nǎlǐ? Where is the dog?

它在桌子下（面）。 Tā zài zhuōzi xià(miàn). It is under the table.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

明天天气怎么样？ Míngtiān tiānqì zěnme yàng? What is the weather like, tomorrow?
How is the weather, tomorrow?

明天多云。 Míngtiān duōyún. It will be cloudy tomorrow.

明天王小姐会来吗？ Míngtiān Wang xiǎojiě huì lái ma? Will Miss Wang come, tomorrow?

是的，她会来。 Shì de, tā huì lái. Yes，she will come tomorrow.

你会说西班牙语吗？ Nǐ huì shuō xībānyá yǔ ma? Can you speak Spanish?/Do you speak Spanish?

（不，）我不会说（西班牙语）。 (Bù,) wǒ bù huì shuō (xībānyá yǔ). (No,) I cannot speak (Spanish)./No, I do not speak (Spanish).

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

（我喜欢吃）苹果和梨。 (Wǒ xǐhuān chī) píngguǒ hé lí. (I like eating) apples and pears.

你喜欢吃什么水果和蔬菜？ Nǐ xǐhuān chī shénme shuǐguǒ hé shūcài? What fruits and vegetables do you like eating?

（我喜欢吃）苹果，香蕉，西红柿和生菜 (Wǒ xǐhuān chī) píngguǒ, xiāngjiāo, xīhóngshì hé shēngcài. (I like eating) apples, bananas, tomatoes and lettuces.

你喜欢吃什么水果和蔬菜？ Nǐ xǐhuān chī shénme shuǐguǒ hé shūcài? What fruits and vegetables do you like eating?

我喜欢吃苹果和西红柿，因为它们对我（的）身体好。 Wǒ xǐhuān chī píngguǒ hé xīhóngshì, yīnwèi tāmen duì wǒ (de) shēntǐ hǎo. (I like eating) apples and tomatoes, because they are good for my body.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

Formula: 一起 + verb + 的

他是谁？ Tā shì shéi? Who is he?

他是我中文课的（同班）同学。今年我们一起上中文课的。 Tā shì wǒ zhōngwén kè de (tóngbān) tóngxué. Jīnnián wǒmen yìqǐ shàng zhōngwén kè de. He is my classmate of Chinese class. We have Chinese lessons together this year.

Formula: Verb (phrase) + 得 + adj (phrase)

你中文学得怎么样（了）？ Nǐ zhōngwén xué de zěnme yàng (le)? How is your Chinese learning going?

（我中文）（学得）很好。/（我中文）（学得）马马虎虎。 (Wǒ zhōngwén) (xué de) hěn hǎo./ (Wǒ zhōngwén) (xué de) mǎmǎhǔhǔ. (My Chinese) (learning is going) well./ (My Chinese) (learning is going) so-so.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

爸爸，你看见我的衣服了吗？ Bàba, nǐ kànjiàn wǒ de yīfú le ma? Dad, did you see my clothes?

（没有，）我没（有）看见。你是不是丢在学校了？ (Méiyǒu,) Wǒ méi(yǒu) kànjiàn. Nǐ shì bù shì diū zài xuéxiào le? No, I did not. Did you lose it at school?

我不知道。 Wǒ bù zhīdào. I don’t know.

爸爸，你看见我的衣服了吗？ Bàba, nǐ kànjiàn wǒ de yīfú le ma? Dad, did you see my clothes?

（是的，）（我看见了，）它在桌子上。 (Shì de,) (wǒ kànjiàn le,) tā zài zhuōzi shàng. (Yes,) (I saw it.) It it on the table/desk.

这（靴子）是谁的？ Zhè (xuēzi) shì shéi de? Whose boots are these?

（这）（靴子）是我的。/ 我不知道。 (Zhè) (xuēzi) shì wǒ de. /Wǒ bù zhīdào. These boots are mine. /I do not know.
