句式结构 | jù shì jiégòu | sentence structures

请问我能坐这个椅子吗？ Qǐngwèn wǒ néng zuò zhège yǐzi ma? Can/Could I sit on this chair, please?

能，请坐。 Néng, qǐng zuò. Yes, sit down, please.

小狗去哪里了？/ 小狗在哪里？ Xiǎo gǒu qù nǎlǐle?/ Xiǎo gǒu zài nǎlǐ? Where is the dog?

它在桌子下（面）。 Tā zài zhuōzi xià(miàn). It is under the table.

Míngtiān tiānqì zěnme yàng? 明天天气怎么样？ :

明天天气怎么样？ Míngtiān tiānqì zěnme yàng? What is the weather like/ How is the weather, tomorrow?

明天多云。 Míngtiān duōyún. It will be cloudy tomorrow.

明天王小姐会来吗？ Míngtiān Wang xiǎojiě huì lái ma? Will Miss Wang come, tomorrow?

Nǐ huì shuō xībānyá yǔ ma? 你会说西班牙语吗？

你会说西班牙语吗？ Can you speak Spanish?/Do you speak Spanish?

No，I cannot speak Spanish./No, I do not speak Spanish.

Lesson 6

主题 | zhǔtí | subject

这是谁的？ Zhè shì shéi de? Whose is this?

词语 | cíyǔ | words

chinese pinyin english
有空 yǒu kòng have time
没空 méikòng not free/have no time
参加 cānjiā take part in/attend
安排 ān pái arrange/arrangement
对不起 duìbùqǐ sorry
邀请 yāoqǐng to invite
来 lái to come
去 qù to go
没关系 méiguānxì no problem/no worries
接（某人） jiē (mǒurén) to pick (someone) up
生日聚会 shēngrì jù huì birthday party
情柬 qǐngjiǎn invitation
站 zhàn stop/station

句子 | jùzi | sentences

你这个星期六有空吗？我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。 Nǐ zhège xīngqī liù yǒu kòng ma? Wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì. Will you be free this Saturday? I want to invite you to attend my birthday party.

（是的，）我有空。谢谢你的生日聚会邀请。我（很）高兴接受你的邀请。 (Shì de,) wǒ yǒu kòng. Xièxie nǐ de shēngrì jùhuì yāoqǐng. Wǒ (hěn) gāoxìng jiēshòu nǐ de yāoqǐng. Yes, I will be free. Thanks for the invitation to your birthday party. I am (very) happy to accept your invitation.

你这个周六有空吗？我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。 Nǐ zhège zhōu liù yǒu kòng ma? Wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì. Will you be free this Saturday? I want to invite you to attend my birthday party.

对不起，我没（有）空，我有安排。谢谢你的生日聚会邀请。 Duìbùqī, wǒ méi (yǒu) kòng, wǒ yǒu ānpái. Xièxie nǐ de shēngrì jùhuì yāoqǐng. No, I am sorry that I will not be free and I have an arrangement. Thanks for the invitation to your birthday party.
