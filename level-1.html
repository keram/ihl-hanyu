句子 | yǔzi | sentences

你的生日是几月几号？ Nǐ de shēngrì jǐ yuè jǐ hào? When is his/her birthday?

我的生日是一月二十日。 Tā de shēngrì shì yī yuè èrshí rì. His/her birthday is on 20th of January.

他/她的生日是几月几日？ Tā de shēngrì jǐ yuè jǐ rì? When is his/her birthday?

他/她的生日是一月二十号。 Tā de shēngrì shì yī yuè èrshí hào. His/her birthday is on 20th of January.

祝你生日快乐！ Zhù nǐ shēngrì kuài lè! Happy birthday to you!

Table 4: 时间 | shíjiān | time
chinese pinyin english
作天 zuótiān yesterday

点 diǎn o'clock/hour

句子 | yǔzi | sentences

Formula: subject + number + 岁(suì)。

你多大？/ 你几岁？ Nǐ duōdà? Nǐ jǐ suì? How old are you?

我是二十五岁。 Wǒ shì èrshíwǔ suì. I am 25 years old.

今天是星期几？ Jīntiān shì xīngqī jǐ? What day is it today?

今天是星期五。 Jīntiān shì xīngqī wǔ. It is Friday today.

现在几点？ Xiànzài jǐ diǎn? What time is it now?

现在七点。 Xiànzài qī diǎn. It is seven o'clock.

你几点下课？ Nǐ jǐdiǎn xià kè? When do you finish the lesson?

我八点半下课。 Wǒ bā diǎn bàn xià kè. I finish the lesson at 8:30pm.

Note: the preposition “at” ect. in English, when it is used before time, it does not need to be translated in Chinese.
So does the word do/did etc. in the questions in English.

时间和职业 Shíjiān hé zhíyè Time and occupation

chinese pinyin english

句子 - 时间 | yǔzi - shíjiān | sentences - time

你七点（开始）上班。 Wǒ qī diǎn (kāishǐ) shàngbān. What time do you start to work?

我七点（开始）上班。 Wǒ qī diǎn (kāishǐ) shàngban. I start to work at seven o'clock.

你几点下班？ Nǐ jǐ diǎn xià bān? What time do you finish work?

我下午六点半下班。 Wǒ xià wǔ liù diǎn bàn xià bān. I finish work at 6:30pm.

句子 - 职业 | yǔzi - zhíyè | sentences - occupation

你的职业是什么？ Nǐ de zhíyè shì shénme? What is your occupation?

我的职业是教师。 Wǒ de zhíyè shì jiàoshī. My occupation is a teacher.

你做什么工作？ Nǐ zuò shénme gōngzuò? What do you do?

我（的工作）是教师。 Wǒ (de gōngzuò) shì jiàoshī. My occupation is a teacher./ I am a techer.
