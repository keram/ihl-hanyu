句式结构 (jù shì jiégòu) Sentence structures

你叫什么（名）？ Nǐ jiào shénme (míng)? What is your name?

我叫 ...... 。 Wǒ jiào ..... . I am called/named ..... .

你好吗？ Nǐ hǎo ma? How are you?

我很好。你呢？ Wǒ hěn hǎo. Nǐ ne? I am very good/well. And you?

Formula: Declarative sentence + 吗？

你好！ Nǐ hǎo! Hello!

我叫 ... 。你叫什么？（你呢？） Wǒ jiào ... . Nǐ jiào shénme? (Nǐ ne?) My name is ... . What is your name? (And yours?)

我叫 ... 。 Wǒ jiào ... . My name is ... .

你好吗？ Nǐ hǎo ma? How are you?

我很好。谢谢。 Wǒ hěn hǎo. Xièxiè. I am good. Thank you.

谢谢。 Xièxiè. Thank you.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences

Formula: subject + number + 岁(suì)。

你多大？/ 你几岁？ Nǐ duōdà? Nǐ jǐ suì? How old are you?

我二十五岁。 Wǒ èrshíwǔ suì. I am 25 years old.

今天是星期几？ Jīntiān shì xīngqī jǐ? What day is it today?

今天是星期五。 Jīntiān shì xīngqī wǔ. It is Friday today.

现在几点？ Xiànzài jǐ diǎn? What time is it now?

现在七点。 Xiànzài qī diǎn. It is seven o'clock.

你几点下课？ Nǐ jǐdiǎn xià kè? When do you finish the lesson?

我八点半下课。 Wǒ bā diǎn bàn xià kè. I finish the lesson at 8:30pm.

Note: the preposition “at” ect. in English, when it is used before time, it does not need to be translated in Chinese. So does the word do/did etc. in the questions in English.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences - 时间 (shíjiān) time

情问，现在几点（钟）？ Qǐngwèn, xiànzài jǐ diǎn(zhōng)? Excuse me, what is the time now?

你几点（开始）上班。 Nǐ jǐ diǎn (kāishǐ) shàngbān. What time do you start to work?

我七点（开始）上班。 Wǒ qī diǎn (kāishǐ) shàngban. I start to work at seven o'clock.

你几点下班？ Nǐ jǐ diǎn xià bān? What time do you finish work?

我下午六点半下班。 Wǒ xià wǔ liù diǎn bàn xià bān. I finish work at 6:30pm.

句子 (jùzi) Sentences - 职业 (zhíyè) occupation

你的职业是什么？ Nǐ de zhíyè shì shénme? What is your occupation?

我的职业是教师。 Wǒ de zhíyè shì jiàoshī. My occupation is a teacher.

你做什么工作？ Nǐ zuò shénme gōngzuò? What do you do?

我（的工作）是教师。 Wǒ (de gōngzuò) shì jiàoshī. My occupation is a teacher./ I am a techer.
